About Me

Hi! Welcome to my webpage. I am an aspiring coder working to build my skills and acquired a job as a fulltime web developer. I am originally from Atlanta Georgia, attended undergrade at Georgia Tech and currently a part of the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp to develop my technical understanding and programming skills. I am currently employed as a Technical Account Manager for a company that developes accounting software for asset managerment companies. I currently work as an intermediary between our client needs and the programming team we have.

In my free time I like to read old english novels, hike, watch movies, and travel. My favorite movie is Forrest Gump and I am a dedicated fan of Game of Thrones. The best description of myself in a work environment is a quiet leader, always working behind the scenes to influence team decisions and constantly working to improve team communication so that everyone is always on the same page and no one falls behind.
